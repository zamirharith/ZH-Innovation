Core Features

Everything needed to manage patrol operations digitally.

01

RFID Checkpoint Verification

Guards scan assigned RFID checkpoints to verify patrol presence and activity.

02

Live Patrol Dashboard

Supervisors can monitor active patrol status, checkpoint progress, and operation flow.

03

Zone Assignment

Assign patrol zones, manage checkpoint areas, and organize security coverage clearly.

04

Automated Reports

Generate structured reports for record keeping, review, and management decision-making.

05

Activity History

Track patrol logs and past checkpoint activities to improve accountability.
